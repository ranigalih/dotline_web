### What is a Handtapping Tattoo?
Unlike modern rotary machines that use electricity, handtapping is a 100% manual, machine-free tattooing method. The artist uses two primary tools:
1. **The Needle Stick** - Carefully sterilized, single-use needles attached to a wooden or bamboo stick.
2. **The Mallet** - A specialized tool used to rhythmically tap the needle, gently driving the ink into the dermal layer of the skin.

### Less Pain, Faster Healing
A common question from our international clients is about the pain level. Surprisingly, the rhythmic tapping often induces a meditative state. Because the needle is tapped directly up and down rather than dragging across the skin like a machine, it causes significantly less skin trauma. This results in minimal bleeding, less swelling, and a generally faster healing process.

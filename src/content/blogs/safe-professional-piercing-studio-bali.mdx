### Anatomy First, Aesthetics Second
We treat every procedure as a surgical art. To ensure your body heals without conflict in Bali’s intense tropical climate, we follow a standard that most tourist shops ignore:

* **Implant-Grade Titanium Only:** We exclusively use ASTM F-136 Titanium—the same material used in medical implants. It’s bio-compatible, meaning your body won't fight it.
* **The No-Gun Rule:** A piercing gun is a blunt instrument. We only use precision hollow-needles for a clean, surgical incision. It’s faster, safer, and shows respect to your tissue.

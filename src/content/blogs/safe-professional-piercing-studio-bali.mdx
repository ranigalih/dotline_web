### Medical-Grade Standards
We treat piercing with the utmost seriousness. Our studio strictly adheres to international health and safety regulations. 
*   **Sterilization:** All tools are sterilized using an autoclave.
*   **Needles:** We use exclusively single-use, hollow piercing needles (never piercing guns, which cause unnecessary tissue damage).
*   **Jewelry:** We only provide implant-grade titanium and high-quality surgical steel jewelry to prevent allergic reactions and ensure a smooth healing process.

A successful piercing is 50% the procedure and 50% the aftercare. Our experienced piercers will guide you through a comprehensive aftercare routine, ensuring your new piercing heals perfectly in the tropical Bali climate.
